Placement Confirmation Does Not Constitute An Offer Of Employment

Content

For your placement starting (start date missing) at (site name missing) in (speciality name missing) , please see the below information.

Your employment contract, pay details and rota will all be issued by your employer. Please note that NHS England is not your employer and cannot advise on HR related matters. Further information about your employment terms and conditions can be found on the NHS Employers' website.

Training placements may be subject to change due to educational needs and service requirements.

Your offer of appointment is subject to satisfactory pre‚Äêemployment checks. It is essential that Occupational Health screening, Disclosure and Barring Service (DBS) and other pre-employment checks take place prior to your arrival.

Even if you are in an NHS trust currently, if you are moving employer, you will still need to complete your pre-employment checks before you move to your next placement. Failure to complete pre-employment checks in a timely manner can cause delays to your pay, training, I.T and facilities access which will impact your training experience.

Your Trust will follow the NHS Employment Check Standards.

Their checks will include:

  • Your right to work
  • If you have a criminal record
  • Employment history and references
  • Professional Registration and qualifications
  • Fitness to work

The notification to trusts and doctors in training at 12 weeks is in line with the code of practice (CoP) agreed in conjunction with the BMA and NHS Employers. In some cases, such as when there is delayed allocation to a post due to unexpected circumstances, the trust will work to a shortened timeline.

Under the CoP agreement, you are responsible for providing information when requested, keeping your employer fully informed of any changes to your contact details and start date. Failure to do so may impact on your pay or terms and conditions and your ability to take up your training post. Please note changes to placements now finalised will only be agreed in very exceptional circumstances.

If your Trust has not been in touch with you by eight weeks prior to your start date, please ensure that you contact them directly. You can find a list of NHS Employers here. If you are unable to contact them, please contact your NHS England local office. please contact your NHS England local office.

You may need to ask for the Medical Staffing department when calling trust recruitment departments.
