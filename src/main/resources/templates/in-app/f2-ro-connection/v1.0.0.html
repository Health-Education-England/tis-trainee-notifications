Connecting yourself to your Responsible Officer (RO)

Please note it is your responsibility to ensure you connect to your Responsible Officer (responsible officer missing) and Designated body (designated body missing) via your GMC Online account when progressing into your Foundation Year 2 (F2) placement.

Revalidation will occur 5 years after gaining full registration, F2 is the first year of the 5 year revalidation cycle.

Please go to your GMC Online sign in page and log in using your GMC reference number. Once you have logged in go to 'My Revalidation' click 'Add Designated Body' and enter designated body missing as your designated body.

Please ensure you are connecting to the Postgraduate Dean responsible for the programme you are training in (as detailed above) and not your employer.

Please connect to your RO within 7 calendar days of commencing in your current training programme.

You must remain connected for the duration of your programme this includes any period Out of Programme(OOP) and statutory leave.

Some programmes share a Responsible Officer for a Programme so please ensure you use the details we have provided above as this may differ to what you expect.
