REMINDER - NHS England - - Onboarding to your Training Programme

Content

You should shortly receive an email with details of your new training programme. There are some actions you need to perform to onboard to your programme, which are described below.

Following email notification sent on our records shows you have some outstanding actions.

Please take some time to start the onboarding process by signing in to TIS Self-Service. Once you have signed in you will be presented with a set of actions and notifications to read relating to your training programme. Please complete the actions below, see the Action Summary for further information.

  • Register for TSS
  • Accept Conditions of Joining (COJ)
  • Submit FormR Part A
  • Submit FormR Part B

It is essential that this is completed as part of your onboarding process to your training programme by start date missing. Your progress will be monitored by your NHS England Local Office. Failure to complete these actions may be considered as non-engagement.

Accessing TIS Self-Service (TSS)

TIS Self-Service (TSS) gives you access to your training information. You will need to use TSS to submit essential forms and sign training agreements and complete actions that are presented on the Action Summary.

We have a registered account for you on TIS Self-Service against the email address . To access details for onboarding onto your programme and actions to complete please sign in to TIS Self-Service.

We have no TIS Self Service account associated to your record. Please create one by clicking the link below.

Please ensure you create an account using the email address we have against our records:

TIS Self-Service.

If your email address is likely to change, or you need support signing up on TIS Self-Service, then please ensure that you contact us via or see our FAQ for details.

Once you access TIS Self-Service, you will be presented with a set of actions and notifications to read relating to your training programme. Please complete the actions and read the notification as requested.
